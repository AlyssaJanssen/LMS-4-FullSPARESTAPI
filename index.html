Roller Derby Teams

Roller derby is a skating contact sport played by two teams of fifteen members. Game play consists of a series of short scrimmages (jams). Both teams designate a jammer. The jammer scores points by lapping members of the opposing team. The teams attempt to hinder the opposing jammer while assisting their own.
